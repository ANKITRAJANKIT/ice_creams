Snow Ice Cream


Ingredients

    1 c. (8 oz.) sweetened condensed milk
    1/3 c. sugar
    1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
    4 c. snow
    Sprinkles, for garnish if desired


Directions

    1. In a medium bowl, combine sweetened condensed milk, sugar and vanilla. Whisk until smooth.
    
    2. In a large bowl, pour condensed milk mixture over snow. Stir to combine. Freeze 30 minutes to 1 hour or until almost solid. Scoop into bowls and serve. Garnish with sprinkles if desired.
